2026-07-02 - Bias update learning-rate schedule

Implemented configurable schedules for the ALF expert bias update rate. The default
constant schedule preserves existing behavior, while the new linear schedule decays
from bias_update_rate to bias_update_end_rate over a configured number of
post-warmup router training forwards. The schedule is now wired through AlfConfig,
model router replacement, router metrics, README/docs, and unit tests.
